Frequently asked questions

  • I am under 18 years of age, can I stay without a parent?
  • Every customer should provide his social security number (SSN).
  • When my booking is binding?
  • A booking is binding once it has been confirmed and you have received a booking reference.
  • What is the cancellation policy?
  • cancellations can be made up until 6 pm local time of the hotel on the day of arrival.
  • What happens if i cancelled my booking after the cancellation deadline?
  • If you cancel the room after the cancellation deadline, the hotel is entitled to retain the booking fee/advance payment.
